Squash 🎾 (the sport... not the fruit😅):

I have been playing squash for close to a decade now and I can safely say it is an all rounded sports that I can play straight into retirement to keep fit.

Benefits of Playing Squash

  1. Cardiovascular fitness🫀:
      The fast-paced nature of the game can get your heart rate up and help you burn a lot of calories.
  2. Improved mental health🧠:
      Squash can help reduce stress, boost your mood, and improve your self-esteem.
  3. Weight loss🏋🏾‍♀️:
      Regularly playing squash can help you shed pounds and improve your overall health.
  4. Increased strength💪🏾:
      Playing squash regularly can help increase your strength and improve your overall fitness.
  5. Improved coordination🤾🏾‍♀️:
      Squash requires a lot of hand-eye coordination and quick reflexes.
  6. Improved agility⛹🏾‍♀️:
      Squash requires a lot of sudden changes in direction and quick movements.
  7. Increased flexibility🤸🏾:
      Squash requires a lot of stretching and movement, which can help improve your flexibility over time.
  8. Improved bone density🩻:
      Squash is a weight-bearing exercise, which means it can help improve bone density and reduce the risk of osteoporosis.
  9. Fun🎉:
      Finally, playing squash is simply a lot of fun! Whether you're playing with friends or competing in a tournament, squash is a great way to stay active and have a good time.
